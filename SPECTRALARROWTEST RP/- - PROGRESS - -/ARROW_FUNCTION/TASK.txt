GOAL:
	ADD SPECTRAL ARROW TO MINECRAFT

FUNCTION:
	1. BOW/CROSSBOW/DISPENSER CAN SHOOT SPECTRAL ARROW
	2. SPECTRAL ARROW MAKES GLOW OUTLINE APPEAR ON MOB (10 seconds)
	3. HITTING A BELL WILL GIVE GLOWING EFFECT TO NEARBY RAID MOBS
	4. PLAYER CAN PICK UP SPECTRAL ARROW
	5. SPECTRAL ARROW IS NOT AFFECTED BY INFINITY ENCHANTMENT
	6. BASTION LOOT/PIGLIN TRADES GIVE SPECTRAL ARROW
